The Fishes And I


This is the official website of The Fishes And I.

We make records, sometimes.

We play shows, sometimes.

Read more
We're four friends who enjoy making a glorious racket together - Tug (guitar/vocals), Luke Mackenzie (bass), Steve Roberts (lead guitar), and Steve Gee (drums).

Roaring guitars are the blueprint, with the catchiest of melodies in our DNA.

Initially, The Fishes And I was a performing name used only by Tug. However, in 2017 Tug teamed up with former bandmates to release a debut E.P.

'Turning Tides' has gone on to be streamed over 50,000 times and new songs are set to be released in the near future, honest.
